Welcome to the Level Designer


There are two elements to create levels: Tiles and Items.

Course info, such as name, background, size, and song, can be changed. It is under File --> Course Info. Under file, you can also reset, test, save and load courses, and exit the level designer. While testing a level, press Backspace to return to the designer.

Tiles can be used by selecting tiles --> place tiles. There, you can click on a tile and click on the grid to place it. Also, you can switch tabs to place different types of tiles.

Placing items can be found under items --> place items. Click on an item and then click where you want to place it. There, you can drag the item around or click on it to open it's window.

Item controls


Click --- view window for customizing

Shift --- place mutiple items at once

Control --- locks item onto an 8x8 grid

Delete --- removes selected item (no undo)

You can save and load levels onto your computers cookies, or by exporting as a code. You can share this code with other people so that they can play your levels.
