Super Mario 63 Level Designer Tips: Bouncing Game

Home ControlsUnlockablesInstructionsLevel list Double Rocket FLUDDPausing glitchCreate your own levelMake a bouncing game

Here some tips to make a bouncing game.

1. Make a level as rectangle for blue/green/red/yellow bricks (Yellow bricks are located from "Other" category).

2. Add interactive breaking boxes if you want to break some coin points.

3. Add items as blue coins, 1-ups, and red coins (Red coins are required to gain the shine sprite).

4. Add spinning floating platform.

  • Speed must be at 180
  • Platform count is defaulted
  • Radius must be at 10
  • Pick the floating platform size. "1" small, "2" medium, or "3" large.

    5. Test the level and have fun with bouncing.

    Floating platform is recommended item.
