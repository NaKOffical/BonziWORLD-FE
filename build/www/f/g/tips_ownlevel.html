Here some tips to make a own level.

1. Pick a tile. Jungle or Grass category (Alternatively, you can use floating islands).

2. Add coins to seek on.

3. Add some decorations like trees, bushes, or even more long pinkish forest special item.

4. Draw the tile (Look at 1.) for long heights.

5. Add enemies.

6. Add traps.

7. Test course and don't forget to copy the level!
